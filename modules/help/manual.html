IntelliMeteo User Manual

1. Dashboard Navigation

The main dashboard displays 30-minute interval logs for your Home Station. Use the Search Bar in the navbar to switch between Nigerian cities.

2. Meteorological Computations
  • Psychrometric: Input Dry and Wet bulb temps from manual stations to get RH and Vapor Pressure.
  • Pressure: Convert QNH to QFE or Inches of Mercury (inHg) for aviation altimetry.
3. AgroMeteo Insights

Access historical weather data and perform agricultural-specific analyses to optimize farming practices.

4. Forecasts (TAF) & Warnings

Our TAF generator uses current trends to build a Terminal Aerodrome Forecast. Use the "Plain Language Decoder" below the code block for a human-readable summary.

5. Managing Settings

Access the Settings Gear to save your Home Station and unit preferences (Metric vs. Imperial). These settings persist across your account.

6. Meteolytics

Explore advanced meteorological analyses and visualizations to gain deeper insights into weather patterns and climate trends.

7. Competency Assessment

Test your knowledge of meteorological concepts and applications with our interactive quizzes and assessments.
